- New Opportunity: Move the buyer through the next observable commitment. Entry: Account matches ICP or a qualified opportunity is created. Exit: Next buyer commitment, disqualification, or close outcome is recorded. Next action: Schedule the next buyer-facing action and update required fields.
- Discovery: Move the buyer through the next observable commitment. Entry: Previous stage exit criteria are met. Exit: Next buyer commitment, disqualification, or close outcome is recorded. Next action: Schedule the next buyer-facing action and update required fields.
- Proposal: Move the buyer through the next observable commitment. Entry: Previous stage exit criteria are met. Exit: Next buyer commitment, disqualification, or close outcome is recorded. Next action: Schedule the next buyer-facing action and update required fields.
- Decision: Move the buyer through the next observable commitment. Entry: Previous stage exit criteria are met. Exit: Next buyer commitment, disqualification, or close outcome is recorded. Next action: Schedule the next buyer-facing action and update required fields.
- Closed Won: Capture outcome and learning. Entry: Previous stage exit criteria are met. Exit: Agreement is signed or payment is received. Next action: Record reason, proof opportunity, and follow-up path.
