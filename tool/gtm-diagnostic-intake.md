# GTM Diagnostic Intake Model

Use this intake model as the application field map. Prefer dropdowns for repeatable categories and 1-5 readiness scores. Use text areas only where the user needs to describe company-specific context.

The online tool now uses `intake-schema.js` as the source of truth for rendering the intake form. Update that schema when adding, removing, or changing intake fields.

## Company research

- Company name
- Website
- Industry
- Business model
- Company stage
- Annual revenue range
- AI research notes / prefilled context

## Market and customer

- Primary market
- Target customer / ICP
- Primary buyer persona
- Market urgency score

## GTM readiness assessment

- ICP clarity
- Positioning clarity
- Offer clarity
- Pricing confidence
- Channel focus
- Sales motion
- GTM assets
- Funnel tracking
- Experiment readiness

## Goals and constraints

- Primary GTM goal
- Desired timeline
- Budget readiness
- Team capacity
- Known blockers or risks
