# Guided Weekly Learning Results

## Product decision

Guided Weekly Learning lives inside the Weekly GTM Review. It is not a separate asset because its purpose is to turn current execution evidence into the next controlled test decision.

## What the tool now does

- Recommends one variable to change in the next cycle.
- Shows the specific change beside the recommendation.
- Lists five variables to keep constant so the result can be attributed to the change.
- Allows the user to revise the recommended variable and wording.
- Saves the selected learning change with the weekly review.
- Carries the latest saved learning change into Plan Health and the ordered next steps.

## Decision rules

- Fewer than five attempts: collect more evidence without changing the strategy.
- Ten or more attempts with no replies: change the message only.
- Replies without conversations: change the next-step ask only.
- Conversations without meaningful next steps and no proof use: change proof only.
- Conversations without meaningful next steps after proof use: change the offer only.
- Meaningful next steps: keep the strategic variables constant for another cycle.
- Overdue or missing follow-up actions: change the follow-up process only.

## QA result

- Focused evidence and persistence checks: 11 of 11 passed.
- Four-company content checks: 659 of 659 passed.
- A challenged fixture with 20 attempts and no replies correctly recommended changing only the message.
- The saved review retained five constants and surfaced the learning decision in Plan Health.
