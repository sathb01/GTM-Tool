# GTM OS Four-Company Quality Review

Date: 2026-07-12

## Purpose

Run four synthetic companies through the current live intake and generated plans. Test whether the tool changes its questions, recommendations, scoring, evidence expectations, and plans based on revenue stage, route to market, buying path, and evidence quality.

## Synthetic Companies

### QA - PawPath Safety

- Pre-revenue physical product
- DTC-first with a community-influencer support path
- First target: urban dog owners who walk in low light at least four times per week
- Expected plan: consumer interviews, prototype reactions, price feedback, and a qualified waitlist test
- Must not recommend retail sell-in as the first validation motion

### QA - ForgeLine Operations

- Healthy post-revenue B2B manufacturing consultancy
- Multiple offers and a cross-functional buying committee
- First target: specialty manufacturers with 75-400 employees and urgent throughput pressure
- Expected plan: focus referral-led consultative selling on the core program, improve pipeline discipline, and add a CFO-ready business case
- Must not recommend the large-enterprise transformation segment as the first focus

### QA - BrightNest Refill Co.

- Pre-revenue physical product
- Retail-first, with end-consumer demand as supporting sell-through evidence
- First target: independent natural-home and zero-waste retailers with one to five locations
- Expected plan: buyer interviews, sample review, margin and opening-order validation, and a paid five-store test
- Must distinguish the retail buyer from the end consumer and must not turn this into a DTC-first plan

### QA - RelayMetrics

- Post-revenue B2B SaaS with a small sales team
- One year of weak conversion into the assumed 50-200 employee SaaS ICP
- Actual retained wins skew toward 20-75 employee professional-services firms without dedicated RevOps
- Expected plan: treat the market choice as a hypothesis, compare segment evidence, and run a controlled professional-services test
- Must not simply repeat the assumed SaaS ICP or claim high confidence without reconciling conflicting evidence

## Quality Gates

1. Answers persist after refresh and reopening the saved company.
2. The navigation indicator matches the visible section.
3. Multi-select answers remain visible and retain all selections.
4. Other requires a definition and saves that definition.
5. Recommendations are identified as recommendations and are visible where the choice is made.
6. DTC and channel questions remain path-specific.
7. Report statements can be traced to the correct company, segment, offer, buyer, and evidence.
8. Validation Plan and GTM Plan remain separate assets.
9. Generated lists use readable bullets and preserve grammatical meaning.
10. Scores and confidence do not contradict the detailed evidence.
