# GTM Blueprint Executive Only After Sprint 9.5

This artifact documents the expected executive-only export behavior after Sprint 9.5.

The executive export should include:

- Blueprint Header
- Executive Decision Summary
- Executive Action Plan
- Strategic Insights
- Revenue Impact Priorities
- Remediation Interview
- Prescriptive Recommendations

Sprint 9.5 quality improvements visible in this mode:

- Recommendation inputs are human-readable bullets.
- Placeholder values and internal IDs are suppressed.
- Target-list guidance includes where to find accounts and look-for signals.
- Buyer guidance uses synthesized buyer-role data where available.
- Export controls are hidden from print output.
