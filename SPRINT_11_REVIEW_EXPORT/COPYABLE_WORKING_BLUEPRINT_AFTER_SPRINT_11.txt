Working Blueprint After Sprint 11

Mode: Pre-Revenue Validation
Module: Think Big, Start Small
Purpose: Find your first market, focus your GTM, and generate early revenue.

Core flow:
1. Capture broad market hypothesis.
2. Split the broad market into 2-5 candidate first-win segments.
3. Score each segment across Problem Intensity, Urgency / Trigger, Reachability, Credibility / Right to Win, Validation Speed, and Delivery Fit.
4. Calculate evidence strength separately from score.
5. Recommend the First-Win Market Segment.
6. Generate evidence gaps, Derived ICP Draft, 30-Day Revenue Validation Plan, and next best questions.
